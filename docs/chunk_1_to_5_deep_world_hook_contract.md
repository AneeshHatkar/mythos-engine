# Chunk 1 to 5 Deep World Hook Contract

This contract locks how future Chunk 6 deep world systems connect to existing Chunks 1 to 5.

## Purpose

Chunk 6 will add deep world, ecology, civilization, species, culture, object, and life simulation systems. Those systems must plug into Chunks 1 to 5 without breaking verified code.
